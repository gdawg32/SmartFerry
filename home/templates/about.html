About Us

Welcome to our final year project! We are a team of aspiring computer hardware engineers from Government Polytechnic College, Cherthala. Our team consists of passionate individuals who have come together to explore the potential of IoT and Django. Our project focuses on leveraging these cutting-edge technologies to build a solution that solves real-world problems.

With IoT, we aim to create a system that can collect data from various sensors and devices and process it in real-time. Django, on the other hand, provides us with a powerful web framework to build and deploy scalable applications that can be accessed from anywhere in the world.

We believe that our project will not only showcase our skills as hardware engineers but also provide a valuable contribution to the tech industry. By choosing to work on a project that integrates IoT and Django, we hope to equip ourselves with skills that are highly sought after in the current job market.
